## Rollout Guidance

1. Land token/surface and spacing rules only where the approved authority proves a shared correction.
2. Keep Product Language component definitions unchanged unless multiple approved boards prove a component defect.
3. Isolate any shared Flutter wrapper or theme change in its own commit before screen-specific work.
4. Preserve business/data/controller contracts while changing visuals.
