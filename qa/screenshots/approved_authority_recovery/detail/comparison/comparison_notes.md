## Comparison Result

The current Flutter runtime is not visually equivalent to the approved Detail authority. The runtime is a long single-scroll Material page with a standard app bar, PackLox Header, large carded hero, linear sections, and large action buttons. The approved authority is a dark, tabbed, compact Detail flow with persistent bottom detail tabs and state-specific compositions for overview, gallery, details, value, AI insights, condition, similar items, price history, notes/tags, and actions.
